Music // Creative Expression

Creativity with Lyrics

Guide: Elim Lee // AMI Elementary Submission

Purpose

To encourage children to know that they can make up their own songs and that music is for exploration and play, not just perfection.

Presentation

Model the transformation of a known melody.
"We know the melody to 'Hot Cross Buns'. What if we changed the words to 'Cold Wet Dogs'? The melody stays, but the story changes."
Offer the 5 Paths of Lyrical Creativity.
"We can change words of familiar songs, play freely on tone bars to spark ideas, invite the whole group to rewrite a line, or even write lyrics to a melody with no words yet."
The creative bridge from imitation to invention
"The Teacher is the material here. Your spontaneity removes the barrier to entry. Don't let notation limit them—encourage lyrics to be written down even if they can't yet write music on a staff."
Elim Lee // Singing Module // AMI Elementary
