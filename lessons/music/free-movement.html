Music // Creative Expression

Free Movement

Guide: Elim Lee // AMI Elementary Submission

LessonID: MOV-03

Anchor: TRANSCRIPT_MOV-03

Purpose

  • To prepare the child for skits and dramatic productions.
  • To aid development of rhythm and coordinate efforts with others.
  • To deepen understanding through expressive movement and self-expression.

Materials

Large free space, Music selections, Bells or tone bars.

Presentation

Gather the children and explain the transition to dramatic movement.
"We have learned to control our steps. Now, we will use our bodies to tell a story or show how a piece of music feels."
Present a Dramatic Story (e.g., The Seed).
"Imagine you are a tiny seed in the cold ground. It is winter. Now, the sun comes out. How do you reach for the light?"
Play music and invite interpretation.
"Listen to these bells. Do they sound like heavy rain or a light breeze? Show me with your arms."
"Free movement is the bridge to the Great Lessons. When children learn to express the 'work of water' or the 'growth of a plant' through their own bodies, the scientific concepts become integrated and alive."
Elim Lee // April 1st Sprint // AMI Elementary
