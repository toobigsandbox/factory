Music // Notation & Interpretation

Details of Writing Music

Guide: Elim Lee // AMI Elementary Submission

LessonID: MOV-07

Purpose

To introduce other signs and terms used in the writing of music.

Materials

Tone bars, Cards showing markings used for writing different musical features.

Presentation

Introduce special musical details (Slur, Tie, etc.).
"When we see this curved line over two different notes, it means we play them smoothly without stopping. This is a slur."
Contrast with a Tie.
"If the curved line connects two notes of the same pitch, we hold the sound for the total time of both notes. This is a tie."
"Writing music is a form of communication. These details ensure that the player understands the composer's intent. Encourage children to find these marks in published sheet music."
Elim Lee // April 1st Sprint // AMI Elementary
