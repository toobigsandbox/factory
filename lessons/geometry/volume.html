Geometry // Volume

Volume of Rectangular Prism

Filling the Space Within

Material Analysis

Box of 2cm cubes, large rectangular prism (grey or blue), tickets, labels.

Presentation

Build the area of the base using 2cm unit cubes. Stack them visible to the child.
"First, we find how many cubes cover the floor. This is our Base Area. Now, let’s build upwards to see the height."
"We multiply the floor area by the number of layers. Volume equals Length times Width times Height."
V = l × w × h
"Depending on the district, you might see it as Base Area times Height ($B \times h$). Explain to the child that they are both correct; $l \times w$ simply defines the $B$."
