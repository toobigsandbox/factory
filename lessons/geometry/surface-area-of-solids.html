Surface Area of Solids

Unfolding the 3D Skin into a 2D Map

Material Analysis

Geometric solids (rectangular prism, cylinder, pyramid), large paper, pencil, ruler, tape/chalk for cylinder rolling.

The Lateral Area Roll-Out

Roll a rectangular prism across paper, tracing each side as it touches.
"The word 'lateral' means side. We are tracing all the walls of our prism. Look—it makes one long rectangle."
Lateral Area = Perimeter × Height

Total Surface Area

"If we want the 'Total' area, we must add the top and bottom lids to our lateral rectangle."
Total Area = Lateral Area + 2 × Base Area
