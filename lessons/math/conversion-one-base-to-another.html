The "Decimal Bridge" Method

Explain the concept of the bridge.
"If we want to go from Base 3 to Base 5, it’s easiest to walk across the 'Decimal Bridge' first."
The Decimal Bridge Algorithm

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Translate to Decimal: Multiply each digit by its place value ($base^n$).
  2. Cross the Bridge: Sum the products to get the Decimal equivalent.
  3. Translate to Target Base: Repeatedly divide the Decimal number by the target base, recording remainders from bottom to top.

Student Assessment:

1. Convert 1011 (Base 2) to Base 10. (Answer: 11)

2. Convert 24 (Base 10) to Base 3. (Answer: 220)

3. CHALLENGE: Convert 12 (Base 3) to Base 2. (Answer: 101)
